This mod takes AA50-41 (World War 2 Version 3) and adds maintenance costs. Each units costs 1 PU, except infrastructure & transports are free and battleships cost 2.
To compensate for the higher costs, each playes receives 15 extra PU per turn
The US receives an additional bonus equal to the turn# (for the first 50 turns).
Each power except China receives 1 tech Token per turn.
China receives no tech, but each Chinese area has a 1/10 chance of generating an infantry each turn, even if not controlled.
Modded by Roger Cooper from the existing TripleA xml
